About Us

The College

GPTC Kannur Campus
Est. 1958

A Legacy of Technical Excellence

Government Polytechnic College, Kannur is one of the premier technical education institutions in Kerala, established in 1958. Located in the serene educational hub of Thottada, Kannur, the college has been a beacon of technical education for over six decades.

The college is affiliated to the State Board of Technical Education, Kerala and approved by AICTE. It offers diploma programmes in six diverse engineering disciplines, nurturing skilled professionals who contribute significantly to industry and society.

With a dedicated faculty, modern laboratories, well-stocked library, and a vibrant campus life, GPTC Kannur provides a holistic educational experience that prepares students for successful careers in the ever-evolving technological landscape.

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

"To empower the youth by imparting excellence in technical education and prepare the students as a valuable resource for industry and society by developing effective technical competency."

Our Mission

  • To offer value-added qualitative technical education through effective teaching-learning process.
  • To provide adequate infrastructure to impart finest quality skill education.
  • To include area of specification which will offer interaction with the institution and industry for entrepreneurship.
